10 Best Ways To Trim Your Budget!

  • Walk or take the bus instead of driving, this will also keep you thin!
  • Make your morning coffee at home.
  • Pack your lunch on workdays.
  • Wake up an hour earlier.
  • Make a grocery list before shopping and dont shop hungry.
  • Set up a fixed automatic transfer to savings before you get a chance to touch it.
  • Take cash out for your entertainment budget so you are more aware of your spending.
  • Make a point to cook at home most week nights.
  • Identify your spending weaknesses and look at them from an annual perspective instead of a daily one.
  • Make savings goals to incentivize better budgeting.
